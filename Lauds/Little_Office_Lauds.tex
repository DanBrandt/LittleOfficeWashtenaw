\vspace*{7.5cm}
It is written in the Gospel of St. Mark, that Our Lord, after healing many afflicted with disease for an entire night, rose ``very early, going out, he went into a desert place: and there he prayed.'' And so, in praying \textit{Lauds} we imitate our tireless Saviour, folowing him as did Simon, to center our hearts on God, that we may receive the strength to carry out His Will. O Immaculate Virgin, pray for us that we may overcome sloth and malaise, and join thy Son in prayer and obesiance to the will of the Almighty Father. Amen.
